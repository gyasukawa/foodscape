Growing food has never been easier.

We provide a simple way to get a vegetable garden installed and maintained for a season full of garden-fresh produce.

How It Works

1

You tell us about your space

After you answer a few questions about your space, we'll send you personalized options for the best type of garden for you to grow food. You choose an option, a date and a time, and we'll get to work!

Already have a garden and just want some help?

No problem. Tell us what you need and we'll send someone to help keep it healthy and fresh.

2

We send a gardner your way

Our garden gnomes will deliver and install your garden, from setting up the garden bed to planting your vegetables.

If you select a service plan, our garden gnome will come check up on your garden or be available via text/email to answer your gardening questions

3

You enjoy produce from your garden!

Watch your veggies grow from seeds and starts to full grown vegetables. Enjoy fresh and healthy produce, straight from your home!
